Department: Content
Title: Manager of Integrated Stats
Reporting line: Director of Content
Location: 15303 Ventura Boulevard, Suite 220, Sherman Oaks, CA 91403

Position Summary:

Reporting to the Director of Content, the Manager - Integrated Stats is responsible for building and managing the live statistics team that will create one of the most unique content offerings in the eSports universe. This role will curate integrated statistics plans for each game covered by Azubu and execute a content plan that gives our viewers an exclusive look at, and in-depth understanding of, dynamic, detailed and fluid game and player statistics. This person will connect with key players, set timelines and ensure consistent and timely communication between all departments involved in the delivery of this game-changing content. Administrative duties, such as developing workflow charts, establishing processes and procedures and disseminating information, will also be key functions of this role.

Core Responsibilities:

Team Management (Think quarterback, leading your team downfield)
  • Oversees and facilitates the creation of the Statistics Team for both live and historical data encapsulation; Creates on-boarding plans for new hires joining the team.
  • Liaises with eSports Management and key stakeholders to coordinate scheduling of broadcasters and analysis; raises scheduling concerns with the Director, Content & Statistics.
  • Works closely with the Director, Content, to build strategy around stats content, streamers and partners.
  • Meets regularly with streamers to review performance, support high-quality deliverables and ensure statistics are being regularly reported and updated. Integrated Stats (Think baseball stats-guy meets Vegas bookie).
  • Identify, analyze and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.
  • Work with Director, Content and key stakeholders to determine what data should be tracked and how.
  • Curate global, stats-driven storylines for a multitude of games.
  • Manage all broadcast statistics and decide what is shown during each broadcast; work with each team to determine what to track, how to track, and how to best present statistical content to viewers.
