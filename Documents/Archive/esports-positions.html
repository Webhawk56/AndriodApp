Department: eSports
Title: eSports Manager
Reporting line: Director of eSports
Location: 15303 Ventura Boulevard, Suite 220, Sherman Oaks, CA 91403

Position Summary:

Reporting to the Director of eSports, the eSports Manager manages multiple relationships with the most prevalent teams in the Korean eSports community, team managers, and specific team players. The ideal candidate is a motivated self-starter who is flexible and can adapt quickly to changing goals and objectives in the fast-paced eSports industry. The ideal candidate is a leader with a proven track record of proactively identifying key eSports strategies, anticipating business needs, and implementing solutions across the organization. This person must have experience working with the Korean eSports scene, strong personnel management, motivational skills, and must be able to work within budget while still producing epic experiences for the players of our games.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Searches for new games, talent and eSports events.
  • Builds and maintains long-standing relationships with external partners.
  • Helps to ensure a consistent and high-level of quality for all account broadcasts on Azubu.
  • Works with other departments in the company to source talent, developers, publishers, and broadcasters to grow the business.
  • Provides visibility, clarification, and status on initiatives related to position responsibilities, including monthly updates and in-depth quarterly analysis / progress reports.
  • Assists management of partners through timely and regular communications concerning delivery of projects and/or ongoing service.
  • Coordinates, confers and works with Marketing Department on effective event coordination and promotion.
  • Works with Content Department to create eSports content.
