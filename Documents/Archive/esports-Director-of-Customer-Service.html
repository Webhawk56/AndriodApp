Department: eSports
Title: Director of Customer Service
Reporting line: Chief Operating Officer
Location: 15303 Ventura Boulevard, Suite 220, Sherman Oaks, CA 91403

Position Summary:

Azubu is a leading online and mobile broadcasting network that delivers premium live and on- demand eSports action, global programming, news and analysis. Intensely entrenched within the eSports landscape, Azubu is positioned to drastically grow the eSports ecosystem and raise the bar of eSports broadcasting. Azubu’s global team which is comprised of senior gaming executives and passionate gaming advocates are committed to supporting the growth of competitive gaming through strategic partnerships with players, publishers and brands alike. Azubu is headquartered in Los Angeles, with offices in Vancouver and Seoul.

We’re looking for a Director of Customer Service who will serve as the leader for all support operations activities. This person will have a key leadership role in the department and be responsible for driving customer support excellence and process improvement. Primary areas of focus will be workforce management, triage support, social media improvements, and the implementations of strategic initiatives.

As the Director of Customer Service, you will:

  • Recruit, manage, lead and motivate the customer support operations team.
  • Assume overall responsibility for coordinating, communication, and tracking KPI adherence and compliance.
  • Recommend the most efficient use of labor resources and redeploy as necessary to meet customer support requirements.
  • Calculate labor efficiency metrics and make recommendations for improvement.
  • Oversee the Customer Experience team (including escalations and order fulfillment).
  • Integrate social media (live chat, email, Twitter, Facebook, etc.) into a viable support channel.
  • Develop, build, and expand the organization as necessary to meet departmental goals and objectives.
  • Provide high level communication summaries and presentations for management.
  • Maintain senior-level account relationships.
  • Ensure the accuracy of reporting systems.

As the Director of Customer Service, you have:

  • Deep personal experience as an online gamer.
  • A BA/BS (preferred) with 5+ years’ experience leading a high-performing, multi-channel contact center.
  • A high level of leadership skills with a desire to cultivate leadership and performance in direct reports.
  • Knowledge of contact center workforce management and technology platforms.
  • Knowledge of Salesforce or similar reporting systems.
  • A demonstrated collaboration, negotiation, and conflict resolution skills, with an ability to lead, acknowledge, develop, communication and implement a strategy to ensure compliance.
  • Excellent oral communication and report skills, as well as business correspondence and procedure writing skills.
  • A dynamic personality with the energy, passion and enthusiasm to drive change.
  • A proven ability to multitask and handle competing priorities effectively.
  • Lean, Six Sigma experience (a plus).
