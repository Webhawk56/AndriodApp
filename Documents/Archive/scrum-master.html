Department: eSports
Title: Scrum Master
Reporting line: Director of eSports
Location: 15303 Ventura Boulevard, Suite 220, Sherman Oaks, CA 91403

Position Summary:

Reporting to the Vice President of Product and Technology, and working with their counterpart in Europe, the SCRUM Master is responsible for project (task) managing the West Coast development team to deliver the product features on time and within budget in accordance with the priorities and road map defined by the Executive Producer.

The design and development team itself is spread between Eastern Europe (outsourced) and North America so the Scrum Master – West Coast has to be capable of coordinating with their European counterpart to ensure effective collaboration between the two regional teams. The product itself spans desktop and mobile browser, plus native apps for Android and iOS.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct daily product scrums acting as scrum master for the West Coast teams.
  • Ensure the team is fully functional and productive.
  • Enables close cooperation across all roles and functions.
  • Removes barriers.
  • Shields the team from external interferences.
  • Ensures that the agile process is followed.
  • Clearly identify and communicate priorities to the development team.
  • Clearly communicate product deliverables and associated timelines to the rest of the business.
  • Deliver regular development status reports (burn down charts etc.).
  • Liaise with the Executive Producer to ensure that all features are scoped and prioritized correctly.
  • Liaise with the Creative Director to ensure that all design elements are delivered to the development team in a timely fashion.
  • Liaise with their European counterpart to ensure that inter-departmental development runs smoothly.
  • Manage an agile development environment to accurately reflect the current development status.
  • Identify any resource peaks/troughs ahead of time and related costs to address.
  • Occasional travel to other parts of the organization (LA/London/Ukraine) as necessary.

Who we’re looking for

  • Problem solver and capable of managing multiple tasks simultaneously.
  • High attention to detail.
  • Organizational ability.
  • Knows how to focus on and deliver against priorities.
  • 3-5 years’ experience working with the agile methodology, preferably in the Development Manager or Scrum Master role.
  • Team Player, experience at working in global and distributed team scenarios across multiple time zones is essential.
  • Experience with managing outsourced development organizations.
  • Good inter-personnel and man-management skills.
  • Maintains a level head under all circumstances.
  • Good communicator, both written and verbal.
